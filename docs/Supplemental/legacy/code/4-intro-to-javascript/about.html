Welcome to my New Year's Resolution app!

My New Year's resolution for 2018 is to read more non-social-media content.

I feel like there are a lot of really good books and magazines I am missing out on because of how much time I spend on social media, so this year I am going to actively try to diversify my content consumption.

The cool thing about New Year's resolutions is that they are a social construct, and whether it's being achieved or not depends entirely on the individual. If I miss a day, I can make it up in other ways, so I will try to remember that so I don't stress myself out over tracking :)
